Always the right temperature

Choose the temperature you want. Nice and cool or rather warm, the all-in-one smart bottle will keep it exactly there all day. It's perfect for keeping your cold drink nice and cool during a hot day or for keeping your hot drink warm.

Easy to adjust

With the buttons on the smart bottle you can very easily choose the temperature that you want your drink to be

Waterfilter

There's a waterfilter built inside. This is to make sure that you always drink fresh water. You can simply take water out of the stream, and the filter will take all of the dirt out. A water filter removes impurities by lowering contamination of water using a fine physical barrier, a chemical process, or a biological process. Filters cleanse water to different extents for purposes.

Built in LED

Built in LED, to make it easier to wander around in the darkness, and protect yourself in the wilderness, or to use as a light in your tent.

Handle

Built in handle, to make it easier to carry around instead of holding it all the time while hiking.
